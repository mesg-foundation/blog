Community Update - Early February '20

In early February, the MESG Foundation released a new testnet Explorer, upgraded to Cosmos 0.38, finalized MonetizeYourCode.com, and quite a lot more! Check out whats new:

  • MESG

    Read more posts by this author.

MESG

14 Feb 2020 • 1 min read

Research & Development

  • Launched MESG Network Explorer for visual access of the testnet's data
  • Released MESG Engine v0.18.3
  • Migrated Plasma Guard to OmiseGO’s pre-Lumphni network
  • Updated to Cosmos 0.38
  • Worked on Milestone 2 for Aragon Project
  • Updated error message on process logs. Thanks Vlad Silviu Farcas!
  • Moved the first SDK module to a pure Cosmos module

Marketing & Communications

  • Launched Product Hunt campaign for MESG Network Explorer
  • Expanded and advertised MonetizeYourCode.com
  • Created new Twitter account for MonetizeYourCode (likes, follows and retweets are appreciated!)
  • Released blog post for Aragon Integration
  • Continued growth hacking campaigns
  • Implemented new marketing growth trackers
  • Created a list of resources to promote engagement on MonetizeYourCode.com (Enter your email on the webpage to gain access) Added Aragon Integration to the MESG Showcase
Follow @MonetizeOSS

Partners & Administration

  • Met with Yellow regarding the MESG Token
  • Updated employee policies in the Handbook
  • Worked on new business models and better integration of the MESG Token

Stay up to date

To stay up to date on MESG’s latest developments, join our Forum or chat with us on Discord. Advanced users, head to Github. Plus, if you have suggestions or improvements, feel free to make a pull request. MESG is open source and community-driven.
