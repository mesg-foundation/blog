Community Update - Late May '19

We’ve passed some big milestones in the past few weeks including the launch of the MESG Marketplace Beta, the listing of $MESG on BitForex, plus the release of our new website and ATD Dashboard.

MESG

22 May 2019 • 1 min read

We’ve passed some big milestones in the past few weeks including the launch of the MESG Marketplace Beta, the listing of $MESG on BitForex, plus the release of our new website and ATD Dashboard.

There is much more still in progress, as development never stops at the MESG Foundation. To keep the community in the loop, we will be publishing bi-weekly updates. This is the first of many, so stay tuned.

Read on to learn about our most recent developments:

Research & Development

  • Updated execution database and API
  • Built-out workflow implementation
  • Simplified the output of tasks
  • Integration of workflows in services
  • Researched creating a blockchain based on Cosmos SDK
  • Renamed “MESG Core” to “MESG Engine”
  • Introduced MESG SDK nomenclature
  • Settled ATD accounting and distribution
  • Expanded and solidified the development roadmap
  • Researched deploying services simultaneously on Engine and Marketplace
  • Expanded ATD Dashboard to include new smart contract and circulating supply

Marketing & Community

  • Wrote, designed and deployed the new MESG.com
  • Prepared for the SURGE Hackathon BKK
  • Created presentation for MESG workshop at SURGE Hackathon BKK
  • Built out and refined target audience persona
  • Built out the MESG Messaging Framework
  • Set up Zapier to automate social media management
  • Created a development tutorial video
  • Conducted research and wrote articles re: ATD Dashboard, BitForex, and ATD vs ICOs
  • Improved organization, look and feel of the MESG Blog

Partners & Administration

  • Worked with BeachHead on their MESG integration
  • Managed MESG Token listing on BitForex
  • Met with OmiseGO to find a plasma watcher solution
  • Met with Yellow Incubator to make progress on product-market fit
  • Transitioned team task management from Trello to Monday.com

Stay up to date:

To keep up to date on all of MESG’s latest developments, head over to Github. Plus, if you have any suggestions or changes you’d like to see made, feel free to make a pull request. MESG is open source and community driven.
