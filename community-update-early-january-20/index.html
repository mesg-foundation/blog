Community Update - Early January '20

The MESG Foundation is starting off 2020 with a new Testnet and processes Plus, we're hard at work on a new growth-hacking strategy. Read on to learn more.

  • MESG

    Read more posts by this author.

MESG

15 Jan 2020 • 1 min read

Happy New Year! We're starting off 2020 with a new Testnet and processes that enable on-demand payments, notifications, analytics and more. Plus, we're hard at work on a new growth-hacking strategy, expanding functionalities of highly-active projects through a simple web interface. Here's what we've been up to lately:

Research & Development

  • Released an early version of the testnet
  • Worked on a testnet explorer
  • Created a prototype of the Aragon/MESG integration
  • Built a new Pay-on-Demand process
  • Created a new ERC-20 Notification process
  • Built an ERC-20 analytics process
  • Built a process for ERC-20 notifications
  • Debugged issues on the testnet
  • Fixed bugs on the CLI

Marketing & Communications

  • Worked on a new growth hacking campaign: an easy web-based process creator to white label, expanding functionalities of many products
  • Worked on website template for growth hacking strategy
  • Researched needs and community activity of potential collaborators
  • Improved communication strategy and consistency
  • Created MESG intro video template - thanks to community member Afif Ridwan!

Partners & Administration

  • Met with partner BeachHead regarding ERC-721 and payment processing integrations
  • Had a meeting with Sequoia
  • Met with Yellow regarding ATD and MESG Token

Stay up to date

To stay up to date on MESG’s latest developments, join our Forum or chat with us on Discord. Advanced users, head to Github. Plus, if you have suggestions or improvements, feel free to make a pull request. MESG is open source and community-driven.
