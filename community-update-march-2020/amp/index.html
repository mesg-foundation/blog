Community Update - March 2020

by MESG

2020-04-05


Research & Development

  • Released Aragon app connecting DAOs to webhooks
  • Upgraded the MESG testnet
  • Released MESG Engine v0.19.0  and Engine v0.20.0
  • Updated to Cosmos v0.38.2 and Tendermint v0.33.2
  • Built new Zapier, Telegram, Twitter and Twilio services
  • Added start/stop functionalities for running multiple services and/or processes
  • Improved process filters
  • Improved Cosmos messages
  • Worked on improving the CLI for local development
  • Moved runner management from the Engine to the CLI
  • Worked on process of publishing of resources to the testnet
  • Simplified the CLI for a better UX

Marketing & Communications

  • Launched MESG Ecosystem Hackathon - Online through April 26th on Devpost
  • Worked on marketing conversion goals and analytics
  • Released the Late February video update
  • Worked on new branding and marketing initiatives
  • A/B tested various marketing techniques and target audiences
  • Created an advertising campaign for the Hackathon
  • Expanded listings of MESG & MESG events in directories
  • Updated Aragon application showcase page

Partners & Administration

  • Refocused and refined the MESG Foundation’s business plan
  • Met with Lunie about a potential integration

Stay up to date

To stay up to date on MESG’s latest developments, join our Forum or chat with us on Discord. Advanced users, head to Github. Plus, if you have suggestions or improvements, feel free to make a pull request. MESG is open source and community-driven.
